{# --- INIZIO CONTENUTO LEZIONE --- #} {% call info_card('intro', '1. Why Ask Better Questions?', initial_state='show') %}

This module provides an extensive collection of questions designed to facilitate better social and professional communication. Using these questions effectively helps teams:

  • Build Empathy: Understanding colleagues' motivations, fears, and personal history fosters a more compassionate work environment.
  • Break the Ice: Move beyond simple pleasantries and encourage authentic dialogue quickly.
  • Improve Team Cohesion: Shared vulnerability and humor strengthen bonds and trust.

Reflection Prompts:

  • Think about the last time you learned something surprising about a coworker. How did that change your working relationship?
  • How can asking a personal question (like "What is your biggest fear?") in a professional setting be beneficial if handled respectfully?
