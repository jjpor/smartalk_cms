{% call info_card('explanations', '2. Explanations', initial_state='show') %}

A lot of

  • Used in positive sentences for a large quantity or amount.
  • Can be used with countable and uncountable nouns.

Examples:

  • There are a lot of books on the shelf.
  • We have a lot of time before the meeting.

Much

  • Used in negative sentences and questions with uncountable nouns.

Examples:

  • There isn't much sugar in the coffee.
  • Do you have much experience in programming?

Many

  • Used in negative sentences and questions with countable nouns.

Examples:

  • There aren't many chairs in the room.
  • How many siblings do you have?

Very

  • Used to intensify adjectives or adverbs.

Examples:

  • It's very hot outside.
  • She is very skilled at speaking Spanish.
{% endcall %}

{% call info_card('speaking', '4. Speaking Practice', initial_state='show') %}

General Topics:

  • Food: How much fruit do you eat each week? Do you cook a lot?
  • Travel: How many countries have you visited? Do you enjoy traveling a lot?
  • Hobbies: How much time do you spend on hobbies? Do you have many books or movies?
  • Friends & Family: How many close friends do you have? Do you see your family a lot?
  • Technology: How much time do you spend on your phone daily? Do you use many apps?

Business Topics:

  • How much time do you usually spend in meetings? Do you think it's too much?
  • Are there many opportunities for growth in your company?
  • Do you think your company spends a lot of money on training or employee development?
  • How much information do you usually receive before starting a new project? Is it enough?
  • Do you work with many international clients?
  • How many emails do you usually get in one day? Do you spend a lot of time replying?
  • How much pressure do you feel at work? Do deadlines make you very stressed?
  • Do you think your company invests much effort in employee engagement?
{% endcall %}
{% call info_card('wrapup', '5. Wrap-Up', initial_state='show') %}
  • A lot of → positive sentences, both countable & uncountable.
  • Much → questions/negatives with uncountable nouns.
  • Many → questions/negatives with countable nouns.
  • Very → intensifies adjectives/adverbs.

Final Reflection Questions:

  • Do you spend a lot of time working, or do you have many breaks?
  • How much stress do you usually experience in your job?
  • What's something you're very proud of in your career?
