{% call info_card('part1', '2. Part 1 – Understanding the Importance', initial_state='show') %}

Objective: Understand why engagement and retention are critical for business success.

Speaking Prompts:

  • Reflect on a time when you felt highly engaged at work. What contributed to that?
  • What are the risks for a company when turnover is high?
  • How does retention connect to employee engagement?

Pair/Group Discussion:

  • How does employee wellbeing impact engagement?
  • How does company culture affect whether people stay or leave?
{% endcall %}
{% call info_card('part2', '3. Part 2 – Assessing Current Engagement', initial_state='show') %}

Objective: Evaluate current levels of engagement in a workplace.

Discussion Questions:

  • How often should managers have one-on-one check-ins with employees?
  • What are effective ways to measure engagement (surveys, informal talks, performance reviews)?
  • Have you ever worked somewhere that failed to measure engagement well? What was the result?

Scenario Roleplays:

Scenario: A usually enthusiastic employee becomes withdrawn and less productive.

Roleplay: Manager → check in with employee.

Reflection: How can early intervention change the outcome?

Scenario: The team has achieved a major milestone.

Roleplay: Manager → recognize team publicly.

Reflection: Why is recognition so powerful for motivation?

{% endcall %}
{% call info_card('part3', '4. Part 3 – Implementing Engagement Strategies', initial_state='show') %}

Objective: Identify and apply strategies to improve engagement.

Discussion Questions:

  • What are your team's unique motivators (career growth, recognition, work-life balance)?
  • How do you adapt your management style for different personalities?

Scenario Roleplays:

Scenario: A team member wants to learn new skills not related to their current role.

Strategy: Offer cross-training, personal development opportunities.

Discussion: How does this influence loyalty and job satisfaction?

Scenario: Several team members feel overwhelmed with workload.

Strategy: Discuss workload management, redistribute tasks.

Discussion: How does this help prevent burnout?

{% endcall %}
