1. Warm-Up Questions

Setting the Stage

▾
  • Do you usually drink a lot of coffee or tea during the day?

  • How much free time do you get on weekdays?
  • What is something you are very good at?

2. Explanations

Understanding the Difference

▾

A lot of

  • Used in positive sentences for a large quantity or amount.
  • Can be used with countable and uncountable nouns.

Examples:

  • There are a lot of books on the shelf.
  • We have a lot of time before the meeting.
