1. Lesson Goal

Why Use High-Impact Language?

▾

This guide focuses on drawing out nuanced vocabulary related to corporate culture, personal impact, and persuasive communication. The aim is to move beyond simple descriptions and encourage the use of complex sentence structures, professional jargon, and idioms where appropriate to elevate your professional narrative.

Focus: Using language that shows you are a strategic contributor, not just a task completer.

2. Phase 1: The Company DNA (Culture, Vision, and Values)

Goal: Elicit descriptive and persuasive language related to the workplace environment and high-level vision.

The Why (Mission & Vision)

▾

Key Question: "What is the fundamental problem your company solves, and what is its ultimate mission?"

Advanced Vocabulary to Use:

Disruptive, proprietary, scalable, market share, stakeholder, value proposition.

Culture & Vibe

▾

Key Question: "Describe the daily vibe in your office. If a new person walked in, what feeling would they immediately get?"

Advanced Vocabulary to Use:

Fast-paced, collaborative, siloed, meritocracy, low-to-no bureaucracy, autonomy.
