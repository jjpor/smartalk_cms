1. Introduction & Key Terms

Lesson Objective and Core Vocabulary

▾

The objective of this lesson is to understand what taking ownership truly means in a professional setting and how to apply practical steps to demonstrate it every day.

Vocabulary:

  • Ownership: Taking responsibility for your actions and work.
  • Accountability: Being answerable for your actions or results.
  • Initiative: Doing things without being told; starting something independently.

2. Defining Ownership at Work

What It Means and Why It Matters

▾

Traits of People Who Take Ownership:

  • Clean up after themselves.
  • Fix small problems or report them.
  • Arrive on time and prepared.
