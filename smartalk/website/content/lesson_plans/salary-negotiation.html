Tips & Techniques

In this section, you will find the fundamental strategies to prepare for a salary negotiation, feel valued in your role, and get what you deserve.

Research is Key

Understand the market value for your role in your industry and location. Websites like Glassdoor, Payscale, or industry reports can provide valuable insights. Be ready to explain why you believe you deserve a higher salary.

Timing is Everything

Ideally, negotiate after you've received a job offer but before you've accepted it. If you're already employed, choose an appropriate time, such as during a performance review.

Practice Your Pitch

Clearly communicate your value to the organization. Highlight your accomplishments, skills, and any additional responsibilities you've taken on.

Be Confident and Positive

Approach the negotiation with confidence, but remain positive and respectful. Focus on the value you bring to the organization.
