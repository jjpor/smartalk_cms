1. Warm-up Game: 2 Truths & 1 Lie

Activity Instructions

▾

Objective: Practice fluency, careful listening, and spotting deception.

  1. Each person prepares three short statements about themselves.
  2. Two of the statements must be absolutely true.
  3. One of the statements must be a lie.
