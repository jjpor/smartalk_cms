Personal Vision Statement

Before you can plan your future, you need to define it. A personal vision statement acts as your compass, guiding your decisions and helping you stay focused on what truly matters to you. It's a short, powerful statement about who you want to be and what you want to achieve.

Why a Vision Statement?

▾
  • It provides a clear sense of purpose.
  • It helps you make better decisions by aligning them with your core values.
  • It gives you motivation and direction, especially when faced with challenges.

Creating Your Statement

▾

Answer these questions to help formulate your vision statement:

  • What are my core values?
  • What do I want my legacy to be?
  • What impact do I want to have on my community, family, and the world?
  • What does an ideal future look like for me, personally and professionally?

SWOT Analysis

A SWOT analysis is a strategic planning tool to identify Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats related to the competition of an organization or an individual.

What is a SWOT Analysis?

▾
  • Strengths: Internal characteristics that provide an advantage over others.
  • Weaknesses: Internal characteristics that pose a disadvantage.
  • Opportunities: External elements that can be leveraged to your advantage.
  • Threats: External elements that could cause problems or harm.

How to Leverage SWOT Analysis

▾
  • Leverage your strengths: Identify how you can use your skills to achieve your goals.
  • Address your weaknesses: Develop a plan to mitigate your disadvantages.
  • Seize opportunities: Identify external factors that can help you grow.
  • Mitigate threats: Evaluate external risks and prepare a strategy to reduce their impact.
