1. Warm-Up Discussion

Setting the Stage

▾
  • How many different ways do you use the word “get” in one day?
  • Do you think “get” is more common in spoken English or written English? Why?
  • What’s a phrase with “get” that you hear often in movies or at work?

2. Uses of “Get”

Main Meanings

▾
  • To acquire, buy, come into possession of
    I got a new laptop yesterday.
  • To become, to change into a state
    It’s getting dark outside.
  • To receive (a present, attention, etc.)
    She got a promotion at work.
