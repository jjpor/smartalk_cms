Modal Verbs

Expressing ability, possibility, necessity, and more.

Introduction

Modal verbs are commonly used in English to express ability, possibility, necessity, obligation, advice, and conditionals. This lesson introduces each modal with explanations and examples, followed by discussion questions to help you practice using them in context.

Modals with Explanations + Discussion

CAN

▾

Used to express ability or opportunity, to request or offer permission, and to show possibility or impossibility.

Business Questions:

  • What skills can help someone succeed in your industry?
  • How can teamwork improve results in your company?

COULD

▾

Used to express possibility or past ability as well as to make suggestions and requests.

Business Questions:

  • What could companies do to improve employee satisfaction?
  • What could your team do to work more efficiently?
