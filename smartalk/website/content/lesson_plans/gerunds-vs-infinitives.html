2. Explanation: Gerund vs. Infinitive

Understanding the Basics

▾

Gerunds (-ing form)

  • Act like nouns (Smoking isn’t allowed).
  • Used after certain verbs (I enjoy reading).
  • Used after prepositions (before leaving).

Infinitives (to + verb)

  • Used after certain verbs (She decided to stay).
  • Show intention or purpose (I came here to learn).
