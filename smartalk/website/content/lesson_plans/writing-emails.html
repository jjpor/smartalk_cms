1. Explanation & Key Points

Email Writing Fundamentals

▾

Strategic communication often happens in email. Writing emails effectively means using the correct tone, clarity, and format.

Key Points:

  • Subject Line: Be clear and specific (e.g., “Meeting Rescheduled: Tuesday at 2 PM”).
  • Greeting: Adjust formality (“Hi John” vs. “Dear Mr. Smith”).
  • Body: Structure with short paragraphs. Be clear and concise.
  • Closing: End with a polite phrase (“Best regards,” “Thank you,”).
  • Tone: Match the relationship (professional, friendly, urgent).
