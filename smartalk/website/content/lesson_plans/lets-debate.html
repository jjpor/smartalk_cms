2. Debate Structure & Roles

Instructions: Three-Person Debate

▾

For each topic, each group member has a specific role. Use the vocabulary provided for each topic.

Person 1: Argue Side A (Pro) – Focus on strong, logical evidence.

Person 2: Argue Side B (Con) – Focus on counter-arguments and weaknesses in the opponent's position.

Person 3: Moderator / Judge – Stay neutral. Listen to both arguments, ask clarifying questions, and decide who makes the stronger, more persuasive points.
