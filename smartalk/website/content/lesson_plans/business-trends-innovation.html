1. Warm-Up Discussion

Setting the Stage

▾
  • What recent business trend have you noticed in your industry?
  • Do you think businesses should always innovate, or only when necessary? Why?
  • Which innovation in the last 10 years has changed your work or daily life the most?

2. Vocabulary

Key Business Words

▾
  • Disruption – Innovation that changes an industry completely.
  • Sustainability – Practices that protect the environment and resources.
  • Scalability – The ability of a business or idea to grow efficiently.
  • Agility – Flexibility to adapt quickly to change.
