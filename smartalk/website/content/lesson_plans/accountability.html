Vocabulary: Phrasal Verbs & Idioms

Key Terms

▾

Phrasal Verbs:

  • Own up to – To admit to something.
  • Follow through (with) – To complete an action or promise.
  • Step up – To take responsibility or initiative.

Idioms:

  • Bite the bullet – To face a difficult situation bravely.
  • Come clean – To confess or admit something.
  • Throw under the bus – To betray or blame someone else.
